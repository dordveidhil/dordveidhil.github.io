    About Dordveidhil

    I established Dordveidhil in 2024 to serve as an adventure into building a personal brand and portfolio. Please select Projects in the menu bar to navigate to both Geospatial and Data Analysis projects.
    Please click Dordveidhil CV to access my current CV.

    Hello, my name is Jillian Walechka-Olson and welcome to my portfolio! Here is where I chronicle my journey in geospatial analysis and GIS as a whole. During my growth, I've provided preliminary environmental site data to Circle K Real Estate/Construction & Civil teams with information for site assesments, worked with the NASA DEVELOP program to analyze urban heat, compared NASA's Soil Moisture Active Passive(SMAP) remote sensing products to in situ soil moisture, and provided maps to inform a stormwater assessment. Currently, I am part of the Strategy & Systems team within Global Real Estate Analytics at Circle K. My team acts as data administrators for Circle K's Development team: ensuring data quality and assisting with the creation of new processes, documentation, and tools for use by the greater team. Personally, I serve as one of the primary administrators for the team's GIS, KLI. I'm continuing to expand my skillset in geospatial and data by pursuing coursework in the following:

    • Google Earth Engine.
    • Python.
    • Esri & QGIS
    • MysQL & SQLite.
    • Power BI & Power Query
    • R
